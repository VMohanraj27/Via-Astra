Interview Preparation

Business Questions

  • Expedia's business thrives on optimizing travel experiences. How would you identify a new ML opportunity within the travel booking or post-booking journey that could significantly enhance customer satisfaction or drive business revenue?
  • Imagine you've developed an ML model that improves flight recommendation accuracy by 5%. How would you quantify the business impact of this improvement for Expedia, and what metrics would you track post-deployment to ensure its continued value?
  • Expedia operates in a highly dynamic and competitive market. How do you ensure that the ML solutions you build remain relevant and effective in the face of constantly changing user behavior, market trends, and competitor strategies?

Machine Learning Questions

  • You have experience with ensemble models and predictive ML. Describe a complex real-world problem where you chose an ensemble approach over a single model, detailing the specific challenges, the ensemble technique used, and how you evaluated its performance and robustness.
  • Expedia deals with potentially sparse data (e.g., new destinations, rare user preferences) in recommendation systems. How would you design a recommendation system or search algorithm that effectively handles data sparsity, and what techniques would you employ to mitigate its challenges?
  • Your resume mentions 'token-optimized staged retrieval' and 'LLM cost savings.' Can you elaborate on the technical details of how you achieved these optimizations in the context of a GenAI system, and what trade-offs you considered regarding model quality and latency?

System Design Questions

  • Design a scalable, real-time recommendation system for Expedia that can serve personalized travel recommendations to millions of users. Discuss the key architectural components, data flow, how you would handle low-latency inference, and ensure high availability.
  • Expedia's data is vast and complex. How would you architect a data pipeline for training and serving ML models that ingest, transform, and manage petabytes of travel data, considering both batch and streaming requirements and data governance?
  • You've built load-balanced, event-driven Azure Functions. How would you design a system to manage traffic spikes and ensure consistent performance for a critical ML inference service (e.g., a pricing model) during peak travel booking periods, considering factors like latency, cost, and fault tolerance?

MLOps Questions

  • You've implemented drift monitoring dashboards and CI/CD retraining workflows. Walk us through your strategy for monitoring model performance in production for a critical ML service (e.g., shipment pricing), including how you define 'drift,' what metrics you track, and your automated response mechanisms.
  • Describe your experience with model versioning and artifact management in a production ML environment. How do you ensure reproducibility and traceability of models from experimentation to deployment, especially when dealing with multiple iterations and feature changes across teams?
  • Expedia deploys models across various services. How would you approach designing a robust and automated CI/CD pipeline for machine learning models, specifically addressing challenges like data validation, model testing, and safe rollouts/rollbacks in a distributed environment?

Behavioral Questions

  • Collaboration is key at Expedia. Describe a situation where you had to work closely with business partners or engineering teams to deliver an ML solution. How did you manage differing priorities, gather requirements, and ensure successful cross-functional delivery?
  • Your resume highlights several projects with significant business impact. Can you describe a project where the initial business requirements were unclear or changed significantly during development, and how you adapted your approach to still deliver value and manage stakeholder expectations?
