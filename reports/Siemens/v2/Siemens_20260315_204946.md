### Machine Learning Questions
- In your shipment pricing model, you reduced MAPE from 12% to 5%. Can you walk me through the specific techniques or ensemble methods you used to achieve this improvement, and what challenges did you face with data quality or feature engineering?

- Design a scalable ML system for real-time anomaly detection in sensor data from industrial machinery. Discuss data ingestion, model inference, and how you would ensure low latency and high availability.
- You mentioned a scalable batch inference pipeline using Spark UDF. Walk me through the end-to-end architecture of this pipeline, including data sources, processing stages, and how you handled fault tolerance and resource management in a distributed environment.
- Describe your approach to architecting load-balanced, event-driven Azure Functions for your NLU-to-SQL system. What specific challenges did you address with cold starts and managing TPM/RPM limits across foundation models?

### MLOps & Deployment Questions
- Detail your CI/CD retraining workflows for the shipment pricing model. How did you manage model versioning, artifact storage, and ensure reproducibility of your deployments?
- Given your experience with Azure and AWS, how would you approach setting up a robust MLOps framework for deploying and managing hundreds of ML models across different industrial applications at Siemens? What tools would you leverage?
- You implemented automated alerting for drift monitoring. Beyond drift, what other key metrics or aspects would you monitor for a production ML system, and how would you configure proactive alerts and incident response?
