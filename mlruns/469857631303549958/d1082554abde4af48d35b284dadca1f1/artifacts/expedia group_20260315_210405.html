Business Questions

  • Expedia Group focuses on improving customer experience in travel. How would you leverage your experience with multi-agent systems or NLU-to-SQL platforms to enhance a traveler's journey, from search to post-booking support?
  • Your resume highlights significant cost savings and efficiency gains. How do you prioritize ML projects that balance technical innovation with tangible business impact, especially in a dynamic and competitive market like online travel?
  • Given Expedia's diverse portfolio of brands and complex data, how would you approach identifying and modeling key performance indicators (KPIs) for a new ML initiative aimed at optimizing travel recommendations or dynamic pricing?

Machine Learning Questions

  • You've worked with ensemble models for shipment pricing. Can you describe a challenging scenario where an ensemble model significantly outperformed a single model, detailing the specific ensemble techniques you used and the rationale behind your choice?
  • Your NLU-to-SQL platform utilizes RAG pipelines and intelligent caching. Can you explain the trade-offs involved in designing a RAG system for a domain-specific application, particularly concerning retrieval accuracy, latency, and cost in a production environment?
  • How do you approach dealing with sparse data, which is common in travel domains (e.g., niche destinations, new partners), when building predictive models? Can you share an example where you successfully handled data sparsity to improve model performance?

System Design Questions

  • Design a scalable recommendation system for Expedia Group that can handle millions of users, diverse travel products (flights, hotels, cars), and real-time updates. Focus on the architecture, data flow, and key challenges like cold start problems and dynamic pricing.
  • You've architected load-balanced, event-driven Azure Functions and implemented round-robin load balancing across foundation models. How would you design a robust, fault-tolerant inference pipeline for a critical ML model (e.g., dynamic pricing or fraud detection) that operates across multiple cloud regions and handles sudden spikes in traffic?
  • Discuss the trade-offs between batch inference and real-time inference for different types of ML models in the travel industry. Provide an example from your experience where you chose one over the other and justify your decision.

MLOps Questions

  • You implemented drift monitoring dashboards and CI/CD retraining workflows for a shipment pricing model. Describe your approach to defining, detecting, and mitigating model drift in production, especially for models impacted by highly dynamic market conditions.
  • Given your experience with Databricks and Azure/AWS, how would you set up an MLOps pipeline for a GenAI application, from prompt engineering and model fine-tuning to deployment and continuous monitoring, ensuring version control for both code and models?
  • Expedia Group deals with substantial technical challenges, including inference problems due to sparse data. How do you ensure the reliability and performance of your deployed ML models in production, especially when dealing with data quality issues or unexpected input distributions?

Behavioral Questions

  • Tell me about a time you had to collaborate with business partners and engineering teams to ensure an ML solution met both business needs and functional feasibility. What challenges did you face, and how did you overcome them?
  • Can you describe a situation where an ML project you were working on didn't go as planned or faced significant unexpected challenges? How did you adapt, what did you learn from the experience, and what was the ultimate outcome?
  • Your resume shows experience with complex multi-agent systems. How do you approach breaking down a highly complex computational problem into manageable components, and how do you communicate technical details effectively to both technical and non-technical stakeholders?
